famous wars

a state of armed conflict between different countries or different groups within a country.

world war 1

world war 1 World War I, also known as the Great War, was a global conflict that took place from 1914 to 1918. It pitted the Allied powers (primarily France, Great Britain, Russia, Italy, and the United States) against the Central Powers (primarily Germany, Austria-Hungary, and the Ottoman Empire). The war led to over 10 million deaths and is considered one of the deadliest conflicts in human history.

world war two

war two World War II (1939-1945) was a global conflict that involved virtually every country, with the main opposing forces being the Allied Powers (including the United States, the United Kingdom, and the Soviet Union) and the Axis Powers (primarily Germany, Italy, and Japan). This war resulted in an estimated 40 to 74 million casualties, making it the deadliest conflict in human history.
